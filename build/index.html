#breaktherules

An initiative aimed at bridging the gap between Student developer ninjas and innovative software companies. We put them in a room together to meet, greet and make internships happen.

Bridging the gap between students and innovative software companies in South Africa

We know a number of good student developers, based on trusted referrals. We look for innovative software companies in South Africa. We get them to meet, greet and make internships happen. Currently launched in Cape Town, with plans to launch in Johannesburg soon. We do various other activities to promote innovation amongst students in South Africa.

Our mission is to promote innovation in South Africa from the roots: students.
